Pangarap Lang Kita – Parokya ni Edgar

About the Song

“Pangarap Lang Kita” is a bittersweet love song about admiring someone from afar — loving a person who feels impossible to reach while accepting the reality that not all feelings are returned.

About the Artist

Parokya ni Edgar is a legendary Filipino band known for their versatility, humor, and heartfelt songs. Their music is nostalgic and recognizable across generations.

Why I Like It

  • I used to relate to the song, but now it’s not just a “pangarap” — I’m living the reality with the man I once connected this song to.
  • It’s been my favorite song since childhood—it gives nostalgic and comforting vibes
  • The Binondo TikTok trend made me appreciate it even more.

My Favorite Lines

“
Ang hirap maging babae, kung torpe yung lalaki
Kahit may gusto ka hindi mo masabi
…”

Ako Naman Muna – Angela Ken

About the Song

“Ako Naman Muna” is a self-love anthem about healing, choosing yourself, and learning to rest after giving so much to others.

About the Artist

Angela Ken is a young Filipina singer-songwriter known for emotional, relatable songs that often focus on growth, healing, and personal reflection.

Why I Like It

  • Reminds me to prioritize self-care
  • Gives a feeling of comfort and emotional rest
  • Empowering message about choosing yourself
  • Soft vocals that are calming to listen to

My Favorite Lines

“

Kakaibabe – Donnalyn

About the Song

“Kakaibabe” is a fun, high-energy song that celebrates confidence, uniqueness, and embracing your own style.

About the Artist

Why I Like It
