Scared to Live – The Weeknd

About the Song

“Scared to Live” is a track from The Weeknd’s 2020 album After Hours. The song talks about letting someone go with grace, wishing them healing, and acknowledging past mistakes in a relationship.

About the Artist

The Weeknd (Abel Tesfaye) is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and producer known for his unique blend of R&B, pop, and alternative sounds.

Why I Like It

  • It’s emotional and comforting
  • The melody is soft and nostalgic.
  • The lyrics feel sincere and mature.

My Favorite Lines

“
I hope you know that I, I’ve been praying That you find yourself
I hope you know that you, you deserve to Be loved, even if it’s not by me.
…”

“Forrest Gump” – Frank Ocean

About the Song

“Forrest Gump” is a track from Frank Ocean’s critically acclaimed album Channel orange (2012). The song uses the metaphor of the movie Forrest gump specifically the idea of someone running ahead to express unspoken feelings

About the Artist

Frank Ocean is an American singer, songwriter, and producer known for his emotional storytelling, soulful voice, and artistic vulnerability.

Why I Like It

  • The storytelling is simple but powerful.
  • The melody is soft and nostalgic.
  • It captures longing in a relatable way.

My Favorite Lines

“
This is love,
maybe I’ve been foolish.
…”

Don’t Smile – Sabrina Carpenter

About the Song

“Don’t Smile” is one of Sabrina Carpenter’s earlier songs that talks about resisting others’ expectations and keeping parts of yourself guarded.

About the Artist

Sabrina Carpenter is an American singer, songwriter, and actress known for her bright pop sound, sharp lyricism, and confident storytelling.

Why I Like It

  • It feels real and vulnerable.
  • The message is empowering.
  • The tone is soft but honest.

My Favorite Lines

“
Don’t smile,
it’s better this way.
