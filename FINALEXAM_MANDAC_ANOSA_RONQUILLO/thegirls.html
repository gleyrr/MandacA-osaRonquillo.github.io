Get to know the creators

Ness, Ianna, and Glaire are three friends bonded by their love of music.

They created this site to share their favorite songs, discoveries, and playlists — and to invite you into the sounds that bring them together.

Beyond recommendations, the site is a living playlist where the trio shares listening experiences, lyrical memories, and collaborative mixes.

Whether you’re chasing new artists, searching for the perfect soundtrack for a mood, or simply exploring what three friends love to play on repeat, this is an invitation: come listen with them, and let the music bring you in.

This is Ness

Ness is a soulful listener who loves vintage records and mellow late-night playlists.

This is Glaire

Glaire is the upbeat curator who finds the perfect song for every mood and moment.

This is Ianna

Ianna brings an eclectic ear — mixing indie gems with modern pop discoveries.
